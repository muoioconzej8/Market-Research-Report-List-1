<p><p>The anchor bolt market is primarily divided into two types: fixed anchor bolts and movable anchor bolts. Fixed anchor bolts are designed to provide a permanent, stable connection, ideal for static applications such as structural supports and foundations. In contrast, movable anchor bolts allow for flexibility and adjustments, making them suitable for temporary setups or applications requiring mobility, like cranes or scaffolding. Both types serve critical roles in construction and engineering, ensuring safety and structural integrity.</p></p>
