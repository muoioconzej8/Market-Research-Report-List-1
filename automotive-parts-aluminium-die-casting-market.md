<p><p>The automotive parts aluminium die casting market encompasses various casting techniques used for producing components. Pressure die casting involves forcing molten aluminum into a mold under high pressure, ensuring precision and smooth finishes. Vacuum die casting uses a vacuum to eliminate air pockets, enhancing integrity and reducing defects. Squeeze die casting applies pressure to the molten metal, achieving dense and strong parts. Semi-solid die casting utilizes partially molten metal to improve fluidity and strength, making it suitable for complex geometries and lightweight designs.</p></p>
