<p><p>The Wet Granulation Equipment market comprises various types that cater to different manufacturing needs. Rapid Mixer Granulators utilize high shear forces and liquid to create uniform granules quickly, ideal for pharmaceutical production. Fluidized Bed Granulators combine granulation and drying in one process, employing warm air to circulate and agitate the powder, enhancing homogeneity. Other equipment includes high-speed granulators and low-shear granulators, offering versatile options for industries like food, chemical, and personal care, each suited for specific granule sizes and properties.</p></p>
