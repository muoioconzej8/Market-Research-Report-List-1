<p><strong>The In-line 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser-based 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment</li><li>PMP-based 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-line 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment is crucial for ensuring quality in PCB assembly. Laser-based systems utilize laser triangulation or structured light to capture high-resolution 3D images of solder paste deposits, allowing for precise height and volume measurements. PMP-based (Moisture Protection Package) systems focus on light reflection, capturing data on solder paste shape and distribution through photoelectric sensors. Both technologies enhance manufacturing efficiency by identifying defects early in the production process, ultimately improving product reliability.</p></p>
