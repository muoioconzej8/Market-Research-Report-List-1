<p><p>The paint gun washers market is categorized into two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based washers utilize water and biodegradable detergents, making them environmentally friendly and safer for users. They effectively clean with lower levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, solvent-based washers rely on chemical solvents to dissolve paint residues, offering strong cleaning power suitable for tough jobs. However, they often emit higher VOCs and can pose health and environmental risks, influencing user preference and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
