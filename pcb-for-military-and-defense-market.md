<p><p>In the military and defense market, PCBs are categorized into three primary types: rigid, flexible, and rigid-flex PCBs. Rigid PCBs offer durability and reliability for static applications, ideal for systems requiring stable support. Flexible PCBs provide adaptability and space-saving solutions, suitable for compact designs and dynamic applications. Rigid-flex PCBs combine the advantages of both, featuring both rigid and flexible sections, enabling complex configurations while maintaining robustness. Each type addresses specific needs in aerospace, communications, and advanced weaponry systems.</p></p>
