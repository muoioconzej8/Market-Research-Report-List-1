<p><p>Electric underfloor heating systems include various market types such as heating cables, electric heating films, and carbon crystal technologies. Heating cables consist of insulated wires installed beneath the flooring, providing efficient warmth through radiant heat. Electric heating films use a thin layer of conductive material to distribute heat evenly across the surface. Carbon crystal systems leverage carbon-infused materials to generate warmth, offering quick heat-up times and energy efficiency. Each option caters to different installation needs and preferences, enhancing comfort in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
