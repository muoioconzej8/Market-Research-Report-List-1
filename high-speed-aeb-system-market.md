<p><p>The High-Speed Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) system market is segmented into three main types: Forward Collision Warning, Dynamic Brake Support, and Crash Imminent Braking. Forward Collision Warning alerts drivers to potential collisions ahead, enhancing awareness. Dynamic Brake Support provides additional braking force when a collision is likely, assisting drivers in emergency situations. Crash Imminent Braking automatically applies the brakes when a collision is unavoidable, reducing impact severity. Collectively, these technologies improve vehicular safety and reduce accident rates.</p></p>
