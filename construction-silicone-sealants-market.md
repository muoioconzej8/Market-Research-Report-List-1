<p><ul><li>Acetoxy</li><li>Oxime</li><li>Alkoxy</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction silicone sealants market is categorized into four main types: Acetoxy, Oxime, Alkoxy, and Others. Acetoxy sealants cure quickly and offer strong adhesion but may emit a vinegar-like odor. Oxime sealants are neutral, making them suitable for sensitive materials without causing corrosion. Alkoxy sealants are also neutral, providing excellent adhesion and durability in various conditions. "Others" encompass specialized formulations that may combine features for specific applications. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
