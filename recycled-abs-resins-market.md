<p><strong>The Recycled ABS Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Post-Consumer Recycled ABS</li><li>Post-Industrial Recycled ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recycled ABS resins market is primarily divided into two types: post-consumer recycled ABS and post-industrial recycled ABS. Post-consumer recycled ABS is derived from end-of-life products, collected from consumers, and reprocessed for reuse, offering sustainability benefits. In contrast, post-industrial recycled ABS comes from manufacturing scrap and leftover materials generated during production processes, allowing for efficient waste management and resource recovery. Both types contribute to reducing environmental impact while meeting the demand for sustainable materials in various applications.</p></p>
