<p><ul><li>Single Stage Module</li><li>Multistage Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermoelectric modules and assemblies market is divided into single-stage and multistage modules. Single-stage modules utilize a single thermoelectric element to provide efficient heating or cooling, making them ideal for applications requiring straightforward thermal management. In contrast, multistage modules consist of multiple cascaded thermoelectric elements, offering enhanced temperature differentials and greater cooling capabilities. This allows for more complex thermal control in demanding applications such as industrial refrigeration, aerospace, and semiconductor manufacturing. Both types serve diverse needs in temperature regulation.</p></p>
