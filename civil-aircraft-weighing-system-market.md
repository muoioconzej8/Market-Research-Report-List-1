<p><strong>The Civil Aircraft Weighing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platform</li><li>Floor-standing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Civil Aircraft Weighing System market is primarily divided into two types: platform and floor-standing systems. Platform systems consist of a flat surface on which the aircraft is positioned for weighing, providing precise measurement using load cells. In contrast, floor-standing systems are integrated units that utilize specialized weighing equipment installed on the hangar floor, allowing for rapid and efficient weight measurement without the need for extensive setup. Both types ensure accurate weight assessments critical for compliance and safety in aviation operations.</p></p>
