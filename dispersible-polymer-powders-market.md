<p><p>The dispersible polymer powders market includes products like Vinyl Acetate-Ethylene (VAE) and Vinyl Acetate-Vinyl Chloride-Ethylene (VAc-VC-E). VAE is known for its excellent adhesion, flexibility, and water resistance, making it ideal for adhesives and coatings.
