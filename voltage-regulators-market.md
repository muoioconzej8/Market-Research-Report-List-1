<p><ul><li>Ferroresonant</li><li>Tap-Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage regulators market comprises various types, notably Ferroresonant and Tap-Switching regulators. Ferroresonant regulators utilize magnetic properties to provide stable output voltage despite fluctuations, making them ideal for critical applications requiring low distortion. Tap-Switching regulators, on the other hand, adjust voltage levels by changing taps on a transformer, offering a more efficient solution for varying loads. Both types cater to different operational needs, enabling industries to maintain power quality and reliability in diverse environments.</p></p>
