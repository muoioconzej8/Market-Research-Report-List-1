<p><p>The industrial endoscope camera market is categorized into three main types: fiberscopes, rigid borescopes, and others. Fiberscopes are flexible, enabling access to tight spaces while providing real-time imaging. Rigid borescopes offer a straight, inflexible design suitable for inspecting larger openings with enhanced durability and optical clarity. The "others" category encompasses specialized instruments like videoscopes and miniaturized endoscopes, which serve specific applications across diverse industries, such as manufacturing, aerospace, and maintenance, enhancing efficiency in inspections and maintenance procedures.</p></p>
