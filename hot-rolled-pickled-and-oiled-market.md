<p><p>The Hot Rolled Pickled and Oiled (HRPO) market features various types, primarily based on the pickling process, which removes scale from hot-rolled steel. Sulfuric Acid Oil involves using sulfuric acid for pickling, offering effective rust prevention and a smooth finish, often favored in automotive and structural applications. Conversely, Hydrochloric Acid Oil employs hydrochloric acid, delivering a faster etching process but requires careful handling due to its corrosive nature. Both methods enhance surface quality, vital for downstream manufacturing.</p></p>
