<p><p>Atherectomy and thrombectomy devices are specialized tools used to remove plaque or blood clots from blood vessels. The market includes various types: Rheolytic devices utilize high-pressure saline jets to fragment clots; Aspiration devices vacuum out clots directly; Rotational devices employ a rotating blade to cut and remove plaque; and Ultrasound devices use sound waves to emulsify clots, making them easier to remove. Each type offers unique mechanisms and advantages for improving blood flow and minimizing complications in vascular procedures.</p></p>
