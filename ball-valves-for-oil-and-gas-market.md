<p><ul><li>Flanged Ball Valves</li><li>Threaded Ball Valves</li><li>Socket-Weld Ball Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball valves for the oil and gas market are crucial for controlling fluid flow. Flanged ball valves feature bolted flanges for secure connections, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Threaded ball valves utilize male or female threads for simpler installations, ideal for smaller pipelines. Socket-weld ball valves are designed for pipes that are welded at the ends, providing a strong, leak-proof connection, often used in high-pressure and high-temperature settings. Each type caters to specific operational requirements in the industry.</p></p>
