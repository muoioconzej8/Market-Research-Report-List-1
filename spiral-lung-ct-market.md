<p><p>Spiral Lung CT, also known as helical computed tomography, is a cutting-edge imaging technique that allows for rapid, high-resolution scans of the lungs. It enhances the visualization of pulmonary structures, making it particularly effective for diagnosing conditions such as lung cancer, pulmonary embolism, and other pulmonary diseases. The technology employs a rotating x-ray system and a continuous movement of the patient through the scanner, resulting in improved image acquisition and reduced scan time compared to traditional CT scans.</p><p>The Spiral Lung CT market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases and the rising demand for early detection of lung cancer.
