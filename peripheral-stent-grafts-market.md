<p><p>The Peripheral Stent Grafts market comprises two primary types: AAA (Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm) Stent Grafts and TAA (Thoracic Aortic Aneurysm) Stent Grafts. AAA stent grafts are designed to treat abdominal aortic aneurysms by reinforcing the weakened artery walls, while TAA stent grafts address thoracic aortic aneurysms, providing structural support in the chest area. Both types enhance vascular stability and reduce the risk of aneurysm rupture, making them vital for managing these serious vascular conditions.</p></p>
