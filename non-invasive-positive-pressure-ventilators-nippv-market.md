In contrast, BiPAP (Bi-level Positive Airway Pressure) machines provide two different pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation, aiding patients with more complex respiratory issues. Both types enhance patient comfort and compliance, driving their demand in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
