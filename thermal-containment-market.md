<p><strong>The Thermal Containment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Aisle Thermal Containment</li><li>Hot Aisle Thermal Containment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal containment market focuses on managing airflow in data centers to optimize cooling efficiency. Cold aisle thermal containment involves enclosing cold aisles to prevent warm air from mixing with cold air, enhancing cooling performance. In contrast, hot aisle thermal containment segregates hot exhaust air from cold intake air, improving cooling system effectiveness. Both methods aim to reduce energy consumption, enhance equipment reliability, and improve overall operational efficiency in data centers by creating more controlled thermal environments.</p></p>
