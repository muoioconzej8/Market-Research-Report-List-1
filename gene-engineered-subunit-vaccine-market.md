<p><p>The Gene Engineered Subunit Vaccine market is categorized into two main types: Therapeutic and Preventative Gene Engineered Subunit Vaccines. Therapeutic vaccines are designed to treat existing infections or diseases and enhance the body's immune response against specific pathogens. In contrast, Preventative vaccines aim to stimulate immunity before exposure to infectious agents, effectively preventing diseases. Both types leverage recombinant technology to create specific antigenic components, promoting targeted immune responses while minimizing adverse effects. This dual approach enhances public health outcomes and disease management strategies.</p></p>
