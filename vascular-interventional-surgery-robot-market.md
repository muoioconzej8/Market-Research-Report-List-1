<p><strong>The Vascular Interventional Surgery Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Navigation</li><li>Magnetic Navigation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vascular Interventional Surgery Robot market comprises two primary types: Image Navigation and Magnetic Navigation. Image Navigation utilizes real-time imaging techniques, such as fluoroscopy or cross-sectional imaging, to enhance precise guidance during vascular procedures, allowing surgeons to navigate complex anatomy effectively. In contrast, Magnetic Navigation employs magnetic fields to control robotic instruments, enabling minimally invasive manipulation of catheters and tools within the vascular system. Both technologies aim to improve precision, reduce procedure time, and enhance patient outcomes in interventional surgery.</p></p>
