<p><p>The Corrugated Tube market is primarily divided into two types: Single Wall Corrugated (SWC) and Double Wall Corrugated (DWC). SWC consists of a single layer of fluted material, offering flexibility and lightweight characteristics, commonly used in packaging and insulation. DWC, on the other hand, features two layers providing enhanced strength, durability, and thermal insulation, making it suitable for more demanding applications like construction and industrial uses. Each type caters to specific requirements based on performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
