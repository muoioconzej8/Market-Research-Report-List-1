<p><p>Vascular surgery electrosurgical units (ESUs) are specialized devices used in surgical procedures to cut tissue and coagulate blood vessels. The market features three main types: monopole electrosurgical units, which use a single electrode and a grounding pad for current flow; bipolar electrosurgical units, which utilize two electrodes to create localized energy, enhancing precision; and vessel sealing units, designed to simultaneously cut and seal blood vessels, minimizing bleeding and optimizing surgical outcomes. Each type serves distinct purposes in vascular surgeries.</p></p>
